Join Us!

Founded in 1983, Bay Area Friends of Tibet is a non-profit, tax-exempt 501(c)(3) organization. BAFoT is primarily supported by the generous contributions of its members and some foundation grants. Volunteers have always played a crucial role in BAFoT's success.

Join us in our celebration of Tibetan culture and help us in the struggle for Tibetan independence. You can support us today by becoming a member, by signing up to volunteer, or by making a tax-deductible donation.

Please note: you can pay your Membership dues through PayPal. To volunteer and/or to become a member, please complete this form.
